# LOKA — The Infinite Manifold

VR-native, AI-orchestrated intent-driven open-world game. Shared map, personal timelines, no predefined end goal. The world responds to player intent. Data generated becomes an alternative history of humanity.

> *"The world that listens."*

## Quick Reference

- **Name origin**: LOKA = Sanskrit for "world/plane of existence"
- **MVP cost**: ~₹1.5 Cr, 6 months, 8 AI-augmented engineers
- **Break-even**: 15,000 paying users
- **Profitability at scale**: ₹25/user/month OpEx vs. ₹999 subscription = ~97% margin at 1M users
- **Key mechanic**: Intent drives world generation, not button presses
- **Anti-P2E mechanism**: Creative Yield Score rewards irreplaceability, not grind
- **Digital legacy**: Tier 5 Elder AI = your trained AI becomes an NPC in other players' worlds after you're gone
